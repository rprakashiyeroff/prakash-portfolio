COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

How long does ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation usually take?+
Depending on the lab's starting point, a gap assessment through to a successful NABL assessment typically runs 4–8 months — faster if documentation and competence records are already in reasonable shape.
Do you work with labs outside Tamil Nadu?+
Yes — consultancy and training have been delivered to laboratories across India, including Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Coimbatore and Salem.
What's the difference between consultancy and training?+
Consultancy is hands-on implementation work — building QMS documents, setting up calibration facilities, preparing for assessment. Training is structured instruction for a lab's own staff (internal auditor courses, QMS awareness, measurement uncertainty) so the competence stays in-house.
Can you support a lab through NABL surveillance or renewal audits?+
Yes — ongoing support is available beyond initial accreditation, including surveillance assessments, scope extensions and corrective action closure.
Which calibration disciplines are covered?+
Mechanical, Electro-Technical, Thermal, Dimension, Pressure, Force, Torque and Mass/Volume — both for setting up primary calibration facilities and for ongoing measurement uncertainty work.
